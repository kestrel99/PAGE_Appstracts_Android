This application is a stand-alone viewer for the abstracts of the annual PAGE meeting. Abstracts can be sorted by Programme item, Presenter or Abstract category, and bookmarked using the star in the upper right corner in the abstract-view. Starred abstracts are subsequently available under the Favourites button. Finally, the search button allows a full search of all abstract content.

The PAGE website is available at
www.page-meeting.org.

The application was custom-designed
for Android by Maxim Schoemaker.
